Push Notifications

Push Notifications

Push brief, relevant updates straight to user devices. There are 2 types:
- classic push (web-push, no IOS inventory)
- in-page push (includes IOS devices & all browsers)

  • high performance
  • large audience worldwide
  • average rates, CPC
Rate starts from $0.003 CPC

Native Ads

Native Ads

Make your ad look like a natural part of the site. Such ads smoothly adapt to the content of the visited page thus making them more trustworthy for users. Types: web & in-app

  • high CTR metrics
  • considered to be more trustworthy ads
  • high rates, CPM
Rate starts from $0.13 CPM

Display Ads

Display Ads

Display your banner ad at the top, bottom or side sections of a website or an app. Use strong graphics to attract your audience. Types: web & in-app

  • variety of ad sizes & image formats
  • good for building brand awareness
  • average rates, CPM
Rate starts from $0.13 CPM

Calendar Notifications

Calendar Notifications

Land your ad straight in the user's mobile calendar app. Such ads are fully adaptive to the calendar view. Reach more IOS users using this type of ads.

  • big IOS inventory
  • content adjustable ads
  • average rates, CPC
Rate starts from $0.003 CPC

Pop-Under Ads

Pop-Under Ads

Have your ads appear under the users opened tab. The creative in pop-under ads is your offer directly, making it easier to attract the user's attention.

  • high user reach-ability
  • big volumes worldwide
  • cheap rates, CPM
Rate starts from $0.001 CPC
